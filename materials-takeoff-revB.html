Vendor Strategy · Unchanged From Rev A

OLNEY IL MARKET

Same playbook: Menards typically takes commodity lumber, OSB, and the concealed-fastener steel roof (their cut-to-length program handles the three shorter planes and step flashing cleanly, and 11% rebate timing matters more now that the package is bigger). Home Depot and Lowe's earn their spot through Pro Desk package bids — always get both, on a ~$75K list they will move. Rev B adds two items worth a specialty quote: the clerestory glazing (16 fixed units — a local glass shop may beat window-brand pricing on simple fixed IGUs) and the now-larger living glazing wall. Glulams still go to the yard desk or an independent lumberyard.
